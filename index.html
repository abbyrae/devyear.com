WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

THE PROGRAM

APPRENTICE

Meet weekly with your mentor and work side by side with an experienced developer.

MENTOR

Be mentored and mentor others. Meet twice weekly with your mentor. First, to set goals for the week and, later, to celebrate. Mentors will work with you one-on-one for the entire year, providing you with resources, inspiration, and accountability. Go out and share your skills.

BUILD

Start by working on small projects to serve needs in the community. Take on increasingly challenging work with the help of expert collaborators. Over the year, build an impressive portfolio of your work.

WORKSHOP

Tinker and learn new skills. Expert guests will join us multiple times per week to help you dive into new languages and overcome tricky learning challenges.

DISCUSS

Exchange ideas and teach each other. Read important texts on the past, present and future of the internet, on hacker culture, and autodidacticism. Discuss critical questions concerning the future of the open web.
